## STEP 3: FONT USAGE RULES

Apply fonts consistently across ALL components:

ANTON REGULAR → use for:
- Page titles (NUTRISI HARIAN, PROGRESS, HOME, etc.)
- Section headers (TODAY'S CHECKLIST, MEDICAL CHECKUP, etc.)
- Navigation labels in sidebar and bottom nav
- Large headlines and hero text
- Button text (MASUK, DAFTAR, UPLOAD MCU, etc.)
- Card titles (ALL DONE TODAY!, NO MCU ON FILE YET, etc.)
- Tab labels
- Badge text
Example: fontFamily: "'Anton', sans-serif"

BARLOW CONDENSED BLACK (weight 900) → use for:
- Metric labels (HUMIDITY, UV INDEX, AQI, etc.)
- Tag/chip text (HIGH, 30 MIN, GYM, etc.)
- Status badges (COMPLETE, UNHEALTHY, etc.)
- Small caps labels and tracked uppercase text
- Tile titles in Quick Check-in
- All letter-spaced uppercase labels
Example: fontFamily: "'Barlow Condensed', sans-serif", fontWeight: 900

BARLOW CONDENSED BLACK ITALIC (weight 900 italic) → use for:
- AI-generated text (MCU summary, nutrition insight, recommendations)
- Doctor notes and medical summaries
- Motivational subtext in greeting
- Insight/tip text in cards
- Quote-style callouts
Example: fontFamily: "'Barlow Condensed', sans-serif", fontWeight: 900, fontStyle: 'italic'

JETBRAINS MONO REGULAR → use for:
- All numbers (temperature, AQI, BMI, weight, HR, etc.)
- Progress counters (5/5, 3/5, etc.)
- Time displays (23:00, 06:00, etc.)
- Stats and metrics values
- Calorie numbers
- Water intake amounts
- Any numerical data
Example: fontFamily: "'JetBrains Mono', monospace"

- Former Orbitron 32px (AQI) → JetBrains Mono 28px

## STEP 6: LETTER SPACING ADJUSTMENTS

Anton has natural spacing — reduce letter-spacing:
- Labels that had letterSpacing: '2px' or '2.5px' → reduce to '1px' or '1.5px'
- Section headers → letterSpacing: '1.5px' max
- Buttons → letterSpacing: '1px'

JetBrains Mono — keep default spacing (0) for numbers.
Add slight negative letter-spacing for large numbers:
- 64px+ numbers: letterSpacing: '-1px'
- 32px+ numbers: letterSpacing: '-0.5px'
